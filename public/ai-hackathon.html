About the Event

"We don't test what you know. We measure how you think with AI."

AI Synergy is Gwalior's premier student AI hackathon — a Two Days in-person marathon at ABV-IIITM Campus, Gwalior. Whether you are a coder, designer, data enthusiast, or simply someone with a big idea, AI Synergy is built for you.

This is not a quiz or a slide-deck competition. You work in teams, tackle real-world AI problems across 6 domain tracks, and build a working solution — guided by industry mentors throughout. The best teams walk away with prizes, recognition, and connections that outlast the weekend.

Participation Rules

Eligibility, Submissions & Judging

Eligibility
Who can participate
  • Open to all currently enrolled UG and PG college students in India
  • Students from any discipline — engineering, science, management, design, humanities
  • No prior coding or AI/ML experience required
  • Age 18+ at the time of the event
Team Rules
How teams work
  • Teams of 2 to 4 members
  • Cross-college teams are allowed and encouraged
  • Each participant can be part of only one team
  • Teams cannot merge, split, or change members after hacking begins
Submission Requirements
What every team must submit
  • Working live demo OR a video demo (maximum 3 minutes)
  • GitHub repository with a README
  • Presentation deck — maximum 8 slides
  • Written project description on the submission portal
What Counts as a Prototype
Prototype = proof of thinking
A prototype is not necessarily code. It can be a Claude/ChatGPT workflow, a no-code AI integration, a live API demonstration, or a Figma mockup backed by AI logic.
Judging is Process-Driven
Reasoning over answers
Judges evaluate how you arrived at your answer — not just what you answer. A team that correctly identifies the root problem and reasons through constraints honestly will score higher than a team with a technically impressive prototype that misunderstands the problem.

Common Questions

FAQ

Q: Do I need coding experience to participate?
A: No. We welcome students from all backgrounds — CS, design, business, medicine, humanities. A prototype can be a ChatGPT workflow, a Figma mockup, or a no-code AI tool. Curiosity matters more than code.
Q: Is this only for engineering students?
A: Absolutely not. AI solutions need designers, domain thinkers, and communicators as much as coders. Any discipline is welcome.
Q: Can my team be from different colleges?
A: Yes. Cross-college teams are encouraged. There is no restriction on which college your team members attend.
Q: Can I participate solo?
A: Solo participation is not recommended. Teams of 2–4 are required. If you don't have a team, check the 'I need a team' option during registration and we will match you via our community group.
Q: What is the ₹500 fee for?
A: It covers your meals for both days (breakfast, lunch, dinner), access to the resource pack (API keys, datasets, starter kits), and the full event experience. It is non-refundable.
Q: Can I get a refund if I cannot attend?
A: The ₹500 fee is non-refundable. However, you may transfer your registration to another eligible student by writing to us at least 7 days before the event.
Q: What is the AI Readiness Certificate?
A: Every participant who completes Stage 1 receives a personalised certificate showing their ARI Score, an 8-dimension cognitive radar, a Human-AI Delta value, and an archetype classification such as Sovereign Augmentor or Critical Validator. It is a specific, shareable credential — not a generic participation certificate.
Q: Are AI tools allowed during the hackathon?
A: Yes — and expected. The test is your judgment about when to use AI, how to use it, and when to override or question it — not whether you can use it.
Q: What must my submission include?
A: A working live demo or 3-minute video demo, a GitHub repository with README, a presentation deck of maximum 8 slides, and a written description on the submission portal.
Q: When are problem statements released?
A: Problem statements are released to registered teams before the event. Teams receive the full problem context at the start of Stage 2 on Day 1.
Q: Who should I contact for queries?
A: Write to us at sangilllence@gmail.com or WhatsApp us at +91 78809 94372. We respond within 24–48 hours on working days.
